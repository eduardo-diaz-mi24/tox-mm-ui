50 Years of electronica

"Inside tomorrow" -- electronica has been presenting the electronics trends of the future for 50 years. It quickly developed into an absolute must for industry representatives where pioneers both then and now present groundbreaking developments. It has always been focused on the future, giving participants a comprehensive look at the international electronics industry. Electronics has always been a driving force in our daily lives. That makes electronica the place where technologies and innovations that change and affect our lives originate. electronica is THE international platform for business relationships and a gathering for all relevant media. It is the only event of its kind that provides a complete overview of the market. No other event depicts the electronics industry as comprehensively as electronica -- from components and systems to applications and services.
